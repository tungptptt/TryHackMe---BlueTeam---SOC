# 1. Junior Security Analyst Intro
## a.Junior Security Analyst Journey 
- Needing defenders who analyze attacks, stop breaches, and keep companies safe.
- Everyday you protect your company from appearing in the cyber news
## b.Daily duties
- SOC 1 - Working in a 24/7 SOC team and mostly reviewing the security alerts together with the team. 
- Needing practice and skills learned : 
  - Monitor and investigate various security alerts
  - Participate in SOC brainstorms and workshops
  - Cooperate with other teams to keep company safe
  - Constantly learn and discover new attacks and defenses
